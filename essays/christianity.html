Motivation

I find it interesting sometimes to consider the following thought experiment about the modern church: if we were to "do it all over" again strictly based on the principles of Scripture, would the new church be recognizable to us?

As a student of computer science, it would seem interesting to explore the Christian faith from "first principles": that is, to find a set of foundational principles from which it is possible to derive a consistent worldview... But what principles would support this purpose? Ought we to invoke the entirety of Scripture, a full sixty-six books? Can the essentials at least—perhaps not covering every edge case—be distilled into a shorter creed? Promising candidates for this include famous creeds such as the Apostle's Creed
