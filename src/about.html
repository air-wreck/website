ABOUT ME

I'm Eric. I currently go to school in Pittsburgh. Before that, I grew up in New Jersey. I guess my life is mostly dominated by math and computers now. Maybe you came here to learn more about me, in which case I'm sorry to say that I'm really not that exciting.

What's the point of a personal website? I don't expect very many people to read these words; if I wanted readers, I would be on Twitter or Facebook instead. I don't expect to have very much interesting stuff to write, and I don't expect to aggressively promote flashy projects to potential employers. I guess you could say that this website has some utility as a landing page for my online presence, but in the end I think I mostly maintain it as a means of self-expression. The romantic view of it is that this is my little message in a bottle that I'm setting adrift in a vast digital ocean. The pragmatic view of it is that it'll almost certainly sink to the bottom of the sea.

Hm, that sounded a bit mushy. Maybe I should avoid writing at 5:00 am and go to sleep instead.
